RocChat is a fully end-to-end encrypted messenger that requires no phone number, no email, and collects zero personal information.

Built on the same cryptographic foundations as Signal (X3DH key agreement, Double Ratchet, Sender Keys for groups), RocChat goes further:

• Username + passphrase authentication — no SIM card needed
• Voice & video calls with encrypted WebRTC
• Group chats with automatic sender key rotation
• Disappearing messages with configurable timers
• Chat folders, pinned conversations, message reactions & replies
• Import conversations from WhatsApp, Telegram, or Signal
• Public channels & communities for broadcast messaging
• Multi-device support via QR code linking
• View-once media, voice messages, stickers & GIFs
• Vault for encrypted notes
• Zero metadata logging — we can't see who talks to whom

All premium features are free forever. Privacy is a right, not a subscription.

RocChat is part of the Roc Family, alongside RocMail (encrypted email) and RocPass (password manager).
